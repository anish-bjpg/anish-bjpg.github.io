What I've Built

TSA Drone Challenge — 2026
My Custom 300mm Quadcopter

I designed and built this thing from the ground up — printed the frame myself, soldered every connection, and spent way too many hours tuning PIDs in Betaflight. Took it to the TSA UAV Drone Challenge and came home with 2nd place.

Flight Controller
SpeedyBee F405 Mini
ESC
SpeedyBee 35A BLHeli
Motors
Feichao A2212 930KV
Battery
Tattu 11.1V 3S 650mAh
Radio System
RadioMaster ELRS
Total Weight
450g with battery
🏆 2nd Place — TSA UAV Drone Challenge 2026

How I Built It

  • 3D printed the frame at 5% infill
  • Soldered motors & ESC (18AWG wire)
  • Wired up the flight controller
  • Installed the ELRS receiver
  • Configured Betaflight & tuned PIDs
  • Bound TX/RX, mapped channels
  • Test flights & FAA compliance checks

Software I Used

  • Betaflight 2025.12.2
  • DSHOT300 motor protocol
  • CRSF (ExpressLRS) receiver
  • Angle + Horizon flight modes
  • My PID tune: Roll P:45, Pitch P:47
